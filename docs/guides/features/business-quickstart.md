# Business quick guide

Set your legal identity and log your first expense in under five minutes.
For the full picture see [Business identity](business-identity.md) and the
rest of this topic.

## 1. Open Business

Sidebar → **Business**.

## 2. Set your legal identity

Legal name, EIN, and fiscal year. This is what shows up on invoices and on
anything your accountant or a state asks for — see
[Business identity](business-identity.md) for why each field matters.

## 3. Add the state(s) you operate in

One row for your formation state, one more per state you're foreign-
qualified in. See [State registrations](state-registrations.md).

## 4. Add people

Employees, contractors, partners — one list, grouped by employment type.
See [People](business-people.md).

## 5. Log your first expense

**Expenses** tab → **New expense**. Not sure which category fits? Check
[Expense categories](expense-categories.md). Migrating history from a
spreadsheet? Use [Expense CSV import](expense-csv-import.md) instead of
entering rows by hand.
