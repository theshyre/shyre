# Proposals quick guide

Draft a fixed-price quote and send it for sign-off in under five minutes.
For the full picture see [Proposals](proposals.md).

## 1. Open Proposals

Sidebar → **Proposals** → **New proposal**.

## 2. Pick the customer

New or existing — the proposal attaches to a customer record the same way
a project does.

## 3. Add line items and pricing

Fixed-price line items, one per deliverable or phase. The total updates as
you go.

## 4. Preview the PDF

Check the branded PDF looks right before you send — this is what the
client actually sees.

## 5. Send for sign-off

The client gets a public link, signs (typed signature + email OTP), and the
proposal is done. Accepted proposals convert into billable projects with
one click, ready for time tracking and invoicing.
