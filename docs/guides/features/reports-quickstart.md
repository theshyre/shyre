# Reports quick guide

Pull your first hours-and-revenue report in under five minutes. For the
full picture see [Reports](reports.md).

## 1. Open Reports

Sidebar → **Reports**.

## 2. Pick a period

Reports default to the last 12 months on a cash basis. Narrow to a week,
month, or custom range from the period picker.

## 3. Slice by project or source

Filter down to one customer, one project, or one time source (manual vs.
agent-tracked) to answer a specific question instead of scanning
everything.

## 4. Read the numbers

Hours and billability come from time entries; estimated revenue and
collected cash come from invoices. Net figures are owner/admin-only.

## 5. Export if you need it elsewhere

Every breakdown that renders on screen has a matching CSV export, for
whoever handles your books.
