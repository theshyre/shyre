# Invoicing quick guide

Turn tracked time into your first invoice in under five minutes. For the
full picture see [Invoicing](invoicing.md) and the rest of this topic.

## 1. Track billable time first

Invoicing pulls from time entries, so make sure the work is logged against
a customer's project (see the Stint and Customers topics if you haven't
yet).

## 2. Open Invoices

Sidebar → **Invoices** → **New invoice**.

## 3. Pick the customer and period

Shyre pulls in the unbilled time and expenses for that customer in the
period you choose. Uncheck anything you don't want on this invoice.

## 4. Review and send

Check the line items and totals, then send. Recipients get the invoice by
email; you can also download the PDF directly.

## 5. Record payment when it lands

Invoices track paid/unpaid status. Recording a payment is built today —
collection (Stripe/ACH) is on the roadmap. See [Invoicing](invoicing.md)
for the full status.
