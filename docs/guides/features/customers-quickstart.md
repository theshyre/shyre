# Customers quick guide

Add your first customer and project in under five minutes. For the full
picture see [Customers](customers.md) and the rest of this topic.

## 1. Open Customers

Sidebar → **Customers**. **New customer** in the top right.

## 2. Fill in the basics

Name is the only thing required. Add billing details now if you already
have them — you can always fill them in later, before your first invoice.

## 3. Add a project

From the customer's page, **New project**. Projects are what you actually
track time against; the customer just tells you who to bill.

## 4. Not billable? Make it internal

R&D, ops, your own product — anything you'll never invoice belongs to an
[internal project](internal-projects.md) instead of a customer.

## 5. Know the lifecycle

Customers move through Active → Inactive → Archived as relationships change.
See [Customer lifecycle](customer-lifecycle.md) for what each state hides
and what it doesn't.
